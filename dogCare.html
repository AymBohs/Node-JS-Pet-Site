Dog Care

  1. Feeding Your Dog
    • Feed your dog a high-quality dog food twice a day¹².
    • The first couple of ingredients should be some kind of meat, not meat by-product or a grain¹.
    • Avoid giving your dog an excessive amount of treats or people food¹.
    • Do not give your dog any chocolate, avocado, bread dough, raisins, grapes, onions, or xylitol¹.
    • Give your dog open access to water at all times¹.
  2. Exercise Needs
    • Ensure your dogs exercise regularly, between 30 minutes and two hours daily².
    • You should also give your dog plenty of exercise by taking it on at least 2 walks a day¹.
  3. Grooming
    • Regular grooming is important for your dog's overall health³.
    • This includes brushing their fur, cleaning their ears, and trimming their nails.
  4. Health Check-ups
    • Regular veterinary check-ups are crucial to ensure your dog's health².
    • Your vet can provide vaccinations, perform routine health checks, and offer advice on care.
  5. Training
    • Training your dog can help prevent behavioral problems.
    • This can include basic commands like sit, stay, and come.
  6. Safe Environment
    • Make sure your dog has a comfortable living environment².
    • This includes a safe space to sleep and play.

Remember, caring for a dog is a big responsibility, and dog ownership is not something to enter into lightly¹.
However, this work will help you to successfully build a bond of love and trust with an important new member of your family¹.
